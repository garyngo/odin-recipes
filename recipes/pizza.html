Pizza Recipe:

Description:

This is a recipe to create an artisan Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Dough

Directions:

  1. Grab a metal bowl
  2. Pour in your flower
  3. Pour in a teaspoon of olive oil
  4. Knead your dough with your hands
  5. Put it in the fridge with a plastic wrap on top of the bowl
  6. After 24 hours pull out the dough
  7. Let it get to room temperature
  8. Put your oven to 450 degree fahrenheit
  9. Grab a tray
  10. Take the dough out of the bowl
  11. Flatten it into a shape of a circle
  12. Put down your tomato sauce
  13. Layer on your cheese
  14. Put your meat onto the pizza
  15. Put your pizza in the oven for 35 minutes
