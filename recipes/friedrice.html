Fried Rice Recipe:

Description:

This is a recipe to cook Chinese fried rice

Ingredients:

  • Day Old Refridgerated Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Peas
  • Defrosted Cooked Corn Kernals
  • Cilantro
  • Chinese Sausage
  • Green Onion
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Directions:

  1. Chop up all the ingredients into small pieces
  2. Heat up your wok at high heat
  3. Put 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil when the wok is hot
  4. After a minute, start with the longer to cook ingredients
  5. Throw into the pan one by one
  6. When you're finished, eat while it's hot!
