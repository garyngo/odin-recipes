Gary Ngo's Steak Recipe

Description:

This is a recipe to cook a steak

Ingredients:

  • Raw Beef Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions:

  1. Rub a little bit of olive oil on the meat
  2. Salt and Pepper your steak
  3. Turn on your stove to medium-high
  4. After a few minutes put a 2 tablespoons of butter
  5. Cook each side of your steak for 1 minute and 30 seconds
  6. Put it on a plate
  7. Let it cool for 3 minutes
  8. Cut into it and enjoy!
